About the Artist

Hello, my name is Vitaliy and I am an Artist. Art is part of my identity and
I am a greatly inspired person with a passion to create. I love carving out
nature and bringing it to life on the piece of wood. Although I have spent
some of my young adult life in Ukrainian School of Art learning about the
fundamentals of art, I believe that art has no rules and no boundaries.
Art is an expression of one’s inspiration, whatever that may be, sometimes
obvious and other times a mystery. My wood carving artwork is primarily
nature and animal illustrations, because I love the simplicity of all living
things that surrounds us. I feel that it's design brings a unique quality to
any room. I love vibrant colors and varying nature background accompanied
with complimentary carved animal shapes. I use my handcraft skills and artistic
abilities to create original pieces of natural art that are built
with quality to last forever.
If I’m not excited to hang a piece in my own home then
I’m not interested in making it at all.
Thank you for your interest in me and my artwork.
I hope you can find something that you love.
